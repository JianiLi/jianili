Jiani Li

Research Assistant at Vanderbilt University

Email, Google Scholar, Github, Linkedin

I am currently a PhD candidate in Computer Science, Vanderbilt University. My PhD advisor is Professor Xenofon Koutsoukos.

My research interests lie in the field of Byzantine resilient cooperation in multi-agent distributed systems, including the concensus, learning and optimization problems.
